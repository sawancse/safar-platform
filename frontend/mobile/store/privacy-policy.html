Safar Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account Information: Name, phone number, email address
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, preferred language
  • KYC Data: Government ID documents (for hosts), encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • Booking Information: Check-in/check-out dates, guest count, payment details
  • Communications: Messages between hosts and guests
  • Reviews: Ratings and written reviews of properties
  • Location Data: With your permission, for nearby property search
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Send booking confirmations and notifications
  • Enable communication between hosts and guests
  • Verify host identity (KYC)
  • Show nearby properties based on location
  • Improve search relevance and recommendations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform safety

3. Information Sharing

We share information only as necessary:

  • Between Users: Host name/photo with guests and vice versa for confirmed bookings
  • Payment Processors: Razorpay for payment processing
  • Cloud Services: AWS for hosting and storage
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Indian law

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

  • All sensitive data encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Passwords hashed with BCrypt (12 rounds)
  • HTTPS for all API communications
  • OTP-based verification for account access

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@ysafar.com.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for location/notifications
  • Export your data

7. Cookies and Tracking

The mobile app does not use cookies. We use minimal analytics to improve app performance.

8. Children's Privacy

Safar is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be notified via the app.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries:

  • Email: support@ysafar.com
  • Website: https://ysafar.com
